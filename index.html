Hello! I'm Joe.

After graduating with my B.S in Computer Science in 2016, I knew I wanted to continue my education. I took courses as a Special Student for continuing studies when I learned about the accelerated Master's program for GIS and Cartography at UW-Madison and fell in love.

While pursuing my master's I began interning at the State Cartographer's Office, where I had the opportunity to work on many of their web applications. The work took all the things I love about programming and combined them with the visual elements of cartography, and I hope to continue such work in the future.

Outside of work I enjoy playing board games, video games, Magic the Gathering, and listening to audiobooks. If you'd like learn more about me, feel free to email me at jbrmarks@gmail.com.
